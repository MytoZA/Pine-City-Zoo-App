THE AMPHITHEATRE

The Amphitheatre is a large open-air venue for performances and events. It is designed to provide excellent acoustics and visibility for audiences, also known as a popular gathering place for visitors to enjoy concerts, plays, and other performances. It features tiered seating that allows everyone to have a good view of the stage.
