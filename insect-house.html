THE INSECT HOUSE

The Insect House showcases a variety of insects and their habitats. It is designed to educate visitors about the importance of insects in our ecosystem and to promote conservation efforts. The house features different sections, each dedicated to specific types of insects, including butterflies, beetles, ants, and more.

Where will you find it?

The Insect House is located near the entrance of the park, adjacent to the Zen Coffee Shop. It is easily accessible and surrounded by beautiful gardens that attract various insects, making it a perfect spot for insect enthusiasts.
