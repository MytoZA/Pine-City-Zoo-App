THE LOST FOREST

The Lost Forest is a lush, green area filled with diverse plant and animal life. It is a serene and tranquil place, perfect for nature walks and exploration. Visitors can enjoy the beauty of the forest while observing various species of flora and fauna.

Where will you find it?

The Lost Forest is located in the northern part of the park, accessible via the main path leading from the Amphitheatre. It is surrounded by tall trees and vibrant greenery, providing a peaceful retreat for visitors looking to connect with nature.
