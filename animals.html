ANIMALS MAP PLACES

ELEPHANTS

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth.

GEMSBOK

Gemsbok are large antelopes with long, straight horns.

GIRAFFES

Giraffes are the tallest land animals on Earth.

GORILLAS

Gorillas are the largest primates in the world.

KOALAS

Koalas are marsupials that are native to Australia.

LIONS

Lions are known as the "king of the jungle".

MONKEYS

Monkeys are primates that are known for their intelligence.

PANDAS

Pandas are known for their black and white fur.

TIGERS

Tigers are the largest species of the cat family.

WARTHOGS

Warthogs are wild pigs that are native to Africa.
