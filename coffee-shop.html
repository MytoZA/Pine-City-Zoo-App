THE ZEN COFFEE SHOP

The Zen Coffee Shop offers a variety of beverages and snacks for visitors to enjoy. It is a perfect place to relax and recharge while exploring the park. The shop features a tranquil atmosphere with comfortable seating and beautiful views of the surrounding gardens.

Breakfast items include:

  • Fruit smoothies
  • Assorted pastries
  • Zen breakfast bowl with yogurt and granola

Where will you find it?

The Zen Coffee Shop is located near the entrance of the park, adjacent to the main path leading to the Amphitheatre. It is easily accessible and surrounded by lush greenery, providing a peaceful retreat for visitors.
