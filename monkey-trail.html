THE MONKEY TRAIL

The Monkey Trail is a scenic path that allows visitors to observe monkeys in their natural habitat. The trail winds through dense foliage and offers a unique opportunity to see these playful creatures up close. It is a popular spot for both photography and wildlife enthusiasts.

Where will you find it?

The Monkey Trail is located in the southern part of the park, accessible via the main path leading from the Amphitheatre. It is surrounded by tall trees and vibrant greenery, providing a peaceful retreat for visitors looking to connect with nature.
